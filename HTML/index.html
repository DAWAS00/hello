Homemade Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish with a creamy sauce, bacon, and eggs

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 150g pancetta or bacon
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper
  • Salt (to taste)

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. While pasta is cooking, cut pancetta into small cubes and fry until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, cheeses, and plenty of black pepper.
  4. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water, then drain pasta.
  5. Add pasta to the pan with pancetta, then pour egg mixture over it.
  6. Toss quickly over low heat, adding pasta water if needed to create a creamy sauce.
  7. Serve immediately with extra cheese and pepper.
